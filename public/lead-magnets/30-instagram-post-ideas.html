Posts 1–10 · Content Pillar
Educational Content
Build authority, earn trust, and attract followers who see you as the expert before they ever buy.
1
🎠 Carousel
"Stop doing THIS in your bench press (5 form fixes)"
Slide 1: hook image showing common mistake. Slides 2–6: each fix with cue + why it matters. Slide 7: CTA.
#benchpress #formcheck #strengthtraining #personaltrainer #liftingtips
2
🎬 Reel
"The #1 myth about soreness that's killing your progress"
30–45 sec. Bust "no pain no gain" myth. Explain DOMS vs productive training stress. End with your training philosophy.
#fitnessmyths #musclesoreness #DOMS #fitnesscoach #gymtips
3
🎠 Carousel
"Squat vs. Leg Press — which one is actually better?"
Slide-by-slide comparison: biomechanics, muscle recruitment, use cases. Position: "both, here's how to program them."
#squat #legpress #legday #trainingscience #personaltrainer
4
🎬 Reel
"3 exercises you're probably doing wrong (and how to fix them)"
Show wrong form vs right form for 3 moves. Keep under 60 sec. Fast-paced, text overlay on screen.
#formcheck #gym #personaltrainer #exercisetips #workouttips
5
🖼️ Static
"RPE 7 vs RPE 9 — what's actually the difference?"
Simple graphic comparing RPE levels. Caption explains why training at RPE 7–8 drives better long-term results than always going to failure.
#RPE #trainingload #progressiveoverload #strengthcoach #periodization
6
🎠 Carousel
"7 signs you need a deload week (and how to do it right)"
One sign per slide. Final slide: deload protocol (cut volume 40–50%, keep intensity). Shows expertise in periodization.
#deload #recovery #periodization #strengthtraining #personaltrainer
7
🎬 Reel
"Cable row vs barbell row — which builds more back?"
Side-by-side comparison. Mention stretch position, load, and use case for each. End: "do both — here's when."
#backday #pullday #backworkout #exercisescience #musclebuilding
8
🎠 Carousel
"Progressive overload explained in 6 slides (beginner-friendly)"
What it is → why it matters → 6 ways to apply it → common mistakes → tracking method. Highly shareable education piece.
#progressiveoverload #fitnesseducation #beginnerlifting #gym #gainz
9
🖼️ Static
"The 4 movement patterns every program needs"
Clean graphic: Push / Pull / Hinge / Squat with 2 examples each. "If your program is missing any of these, DM me" as CTA.
#movementpatterns #programdesign #functionaltraining #strengthcoach #fitness
10
🎠 Carousel
"Why your clients plateau after 3 months (and how to fix it)"
Explains adaptation, periodization, and variation. Positions you as the solution. Great for attracting new clients who've hit walls.
#plateau #musclebuilding #fitnesscoach #programdesign #personaltrainer

Posts 11–20 · Content Pillar
Engagement Content
Drive comments, shares, and saves. Build a community that shows up every week — not just passive scrollers.
11
📖 Story
"Poll: Do you track your sets and reps? Yes / No"
Follow up: DM the "No" voters your free template. Builds conversation, surfaces warm leads, shows investment in their results.
#igstory #poll #fitnesscoach #personaltrainer #workoutroutine
12
🎬 Reel
"Day in the life of a personal trainer (6am–8pm)"
Time-lapse of your actual day: early sessions, programming time, client check-ins. Humanizes your brand. Shows work ethic.
#dayinthelife #personaltrainerlife #fitnessbusiness #coachlife #gymlife
13
🎠 Carousel
"My client [Name] added 40lbs to her deadlift in 8 weeks — here's exactly how"
Before/after progress data (with permission). Programming breakdown. Client quote. One of the highest-converting content types for leads.
#clienttransformation #progresspics #deadlift #personaltrainer #resultscoach
14
📖 Story
"Q&A: Ask me anything about training — I'll answer all today"
Use the Question sticker. Respond to each in Stories. Screenshot best Q&As for a carousel later. Drives DMs and discovery.
#QandA #askmeAnything #fitnesscoach #personaltrainer #gymquestions
15
🎬 Reel
"Expectation vs Reality: Training with a personal trainer"
Funny/relatable format showing what people think PT is vs what it actually looks like. Drives shares + comments. Tag a friend CTA.
#expectationvsreality #personaltrainerhumor #gymhumor #fitnesscoach #relateable
16
🖼️ Static
"Controversial take: Most beginners should NOT train to failure"
State your take boldly. Back it up with 2–3 reasons in the caption. Ask followers to share their opinion in comments. Debate = reach.
#hottake #trainingphilosophy #beginnertraining #fitnesscoach #strengthtraining
17
📖 Story
"This week's client win 🔥 [photo/video with permission]"
Weekly series. Short caption on what they achieved. Tag them (with permission). Creates social proof + keeps your audience invested.
#clientwin #personaltrainer #progress #motivation #fitnesscoach
18
🎠 Carousel
"My training journey: where I started vs where I am now"
Personal story format. Your own before/during/after. Share struggles, turning points, key lessons. Builds deep connection and trust.
#myjourney #personaltrainer #fitnessmotivation #trainerstory #authentic
19
🎬 Reel
"Rate my client's squat 1–10 👇"
Share a clip (with client permission) of a training milestone or improved form. Ask audience to rate and drop tips. Massive comment bait.
#squatcheck #formcheck #personaltrainer #squatdepth #clientprogress
20
📖 Story
"This or That: Barbell squat vs Leg press — which do you prefer?"
Swipe-up poll with image for each option. DM voters a 3-tip follow-up on whichever they chose. Great for warm list-building.
#thisorthat #squatday #legpress #igpoll #fitnesscoach

Posts 21–30 · Content Pillar
Promotional Content
Convert warm followers into paying clients. Done right, promotional posts feel valuable — not pushy.
21
🎬 Reel
"Free 7-day workout challenge starts Monday — comment CHALLENGE to join"
Low-barrier CTA. Creates an email/DM list. Drive comments to beat algorithm. Deliver via Stories or DM sequence.
#workoutchallenge #freeworkout #fitnesscoach #fitnesschallenge #personaltrainer
22
🎠 Carousel
"What my online clients get every week (full breakdown)"
Transparency post: weekly check-in, custom program updates, form feedback, messaging support. Shows value before they buy.
#onlinecoaching #onlinept #onlinepersonaltrainer #coachingservices #fitnesscoach
23
🖼️ Static
"⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ [Client Name] after 12 weeks of online coaching"
Screenshot or styled graphic of client testimonial. Add 1–2 specific results. DM CTA for people who want similar results.
#testimonial #clientresults #personaltrainer #onlinecoaching #fitnessgoals
24
🎬 Reel
"Behind the scenes: how I build a client's 12-week program"
Screen-record your program builder workflow. Show assessment → exercise selection → progressive overload plan. Builds credibility + desire.
#behindthescenes #programdesign #personaltrainer #onlinecoach #fitnessprogram
25
📖 Story
"I have 2 spots left for online coaching this month — swipe up to apply"
Scarcity-based Story. Link to intake form or DM. Repeat for 3 days. Always works better with a deadline or limited spots framing.
#onlinecoaching #personaltrainer #limitedspots #applytocoach #fitnesscoach
26
🎠 Carousel
"The difference between my clients and people who train alone for years"
Side-by-side comparison of outcomes, structure, accountability, and rate of progress. Closes with "Which would you rather be?"
#personaltrainer #coachingmatters #accountability #resultscoach #onlinept
27
🎬 Reel
"What I wish I knew before I hired my first coach"
Story-led Reel about YOUR experience as a client first. Lessons learned. Naturally leads into why you do what you do. Soft promo.
#mycoachingjourney #personaltrainer #coachingworks #fitnesslife #strengthcoach
28
🎠 Carousel
"Here's what's included in my [Tier] coaching package (no fluff)"
Slide-by-slide package breakdown. Be specific: weekly touchpoints, turnaround times, what clients get. Transparent sells better than vague.
#coachingpackage #onlinecoaching #personaltrainer #coachingprogram #fitnessbusiness
29
🎬 Reel
"3 reasons people fail to get results without a coach"
Accountability, programming knowledge, and objective feedback. Each point builds the case for working with you. End: "DM me RESULTS."
#whyyouneedacoach #personaltrainer #gymresults #fitnesscoach #accountability
30
🎠 Carousel
"How I took [Client] from gym-shy to setting a PR in 10 weeks"
Full story arc: where they started, roadblocks faced, milestones hit, final result. Strong narrative = strong conversion. CTA at end.
#clientstory #personalrecord #personaltrainer #transformationstory #coachingworks
